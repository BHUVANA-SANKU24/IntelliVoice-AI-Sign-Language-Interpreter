About IntelliVoice

Breaking barriers with
intelligent communication

IntelliVoice is an AI-powered, browser-based sign language interpreter designed to make communication effortless between deaf/hard-of-hearing individuals and hearing people.

The problem is real: millions of people rely on sign language daily, yet most technology offers little to bridge the gap. IntelliVoice solves this by providing real-time, two-directional ASL interpretation — no apps to install, no specialized hardware required.

♿ Accessibility First 🤖 AI Powered 🔒 100% Private ⚡ Real-Time 🌐 Browser Native
🎯
What It Does
Detects ASL hand signs from your camera in real time and converts them to text — or animates any typed sentence into sequential hand sign images.
🤝
Who It Helps
Designed for deaf and hard-of-hearing users, their families, educators, interpreters, and anyone who wants to learn or communicate in ASL without barriers.
⚙️
How It Works
Powered by MediaPipe hand landmark detection and a custom ASL classifier. Everything runs locally in your browser — no data leaves your device.
